# Hospitalization Hypothesis Testing

## Project Overview

**Apollo Hospitals: Decode regional patterns through hypothesis testing and OLS regression using Python, Statsmodels, SciPy, and Pandas.**

Apollo Hospitals was established in 1983 and is renowned as the architect of modern healthcare in India. As the nation's first corporate hospital, Apollo Hospitals is acclaimed for pioneering the private healthcare revolution in the country.

## Assignment

As a data scientist working at Apollo, the ultimate goal is to derive meaningful and actionable insights from patient-level data. These insights can help Apollo Hospitals improve operational efficiency, influence diagnostic and treatment processes, and map the spread of a pandemic.

One of the best examples of data scientists making a meaningful difference at a global level is the response to the COVID-19 pandemic. Data scientists have improved information collection, provided ongoing and accurate estimates of infection spread and health system demand, and assessed the effectiveness of government policies.

The company wants to determine:

1. Which variables are significant in predicting the reason for hospitalization across different regions?
2. How well do variables such as viral load, smoking status, and severity level explain hospitalization charges?

## Data Description

The `apollo_data.csv` file contains anonymized data for COVID-19 hospital patients and includes the following variables:

| Variable                  | Description                                                                                                                                          |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `age`                     | An integer indicating the age of the primary beneficiary. Patients above 64 years are excluded because they are generally covered by the government. |
| `sex`                     | The policyholder's gender: `male` or `female`.                                                                                                       |
| `smoker`                  | Indicates whether the insured regularly smokes tobacco: `yes` or `no`.                                                                               |
| `region`                  | The beneficiary's place of residence in Delhi, divided into four geographic regions: `northeast`, `southeast`, `southwest`, or `northwest`.          |
| `viral load`              | The amount of virus in an infected person's blood.                                                                                                   |
| `severity level`          | An integer indicating the severity of the patient's condition.                                                                                       |
| `hospitalization charges` | Individual medical costs billed to health insurance.                                                                                                 |
