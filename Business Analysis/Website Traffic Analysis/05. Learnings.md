## Final integration decisions

- Preserve the draft's correct interpretation of Question 3, direct overall click-rate calculation, link-level unit of analysis, and use of Spearman correlation.
- Preserve the official solution's explanatory structure, descriptive statistics, and visual exploration.
- Replace both solutions' repeated merges and biased inner joins with one link-level event matrix that retains zero-event links.
- Replace the official categorical factorization with a valid binary contingency-table analysis.
- Add schema validation, null/orphan checks, low-volume sensitivity analysis, confidence intervals, effect sizes, pageview-normalized tests, and a non-causal business conclusion.
